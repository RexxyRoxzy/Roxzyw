Privacy Policy

Welcome to Stalker, a thrilling Roblox game where players must survive against relentless Nextbots in diverse maps.

Your privacy is important to us. We ensure that all player data, including in-game statistics, are securely stored and never shared with third parties.

When you play Stalker, we may collect basic data such as user ID and gameplay statistics to improve the experience and maintain leaderboards.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as passwords, IP addresses, or private messages.

Our Discord community also prioritizes security. Any data collected within our Discord server is solely used to enhance player experience and maintain a safe environment.

We regularly update our privacy practices to align with the latest security standards.

If you have any questions or wish to request data removal, please contact the administrators via our official Discord server.
